Container Repair

For expert metal container repair services, CJC Welding & Multiservices is your trusted partner. We cater to waste haulers, recyclers, municipalities, and businesses with extensive fleets of metal containers. Our repair services are available at both customer facilities and our own repair shops. By choosing CJC Welding & Multiservices, you can extend the lifespan of your metal containers, saving on capital equipment expenses and avoiding costly replacements. We offer comprehensive repair solutions for various types of containers, including:

• Bottom replacements
• Door fabrication
• Rail replacements
• Welding
• Painting
• Re-labeling
• Complete re-builds
• And more!


Before initiating any repairs, CJC Welding & Multiservices evaluates containers to ensure that the repair costs align with our customers’ repair cost guidelines. If the potential repairs exceed the guidelines, the container is designated for scrap. However, if repairs are feasible, our technicians prepare the surface by employing techniques such as sanding, scraping, sandblasting, and washing.
For containers requiring minor repairs, we perform the necessary patchwork and apply priming and painting. In the case of major repairs, we may fabricate parts or utilize pre-formed components like bottoms and pockets.
Our skilled team handles repairs for all types of metal waste containers and dumpsters, including front load containers, rear load containers, roll-off containers, and more.


Welding containers


Welding containers play a crucial role in maintaining a safe, controlled, and efficient environment for welding processes, ensuring the quality of welds and the well-being of personnel involved in welding operations.
CJC Welding & Multiservices offers comprehensive repair services for roll-off containers, covering the following components:


• Main Body:

Repair or reinforcement of the roll-off container's main body, which is typically constructed from robust materials like steel.

• Roll-off Rails:

Inspection and repair of the rails or tracks on the bottom of the container that facilitate loading and unloading.

• Hook or Cable System:

Evaluation and repair of the hook or cable system that enables attachment to the roll-off truck's hydraulic hoist for lifting and dumping.

• Rear Doors or Gates:

Repair or replacement of hinges, latches, or locking mechanisms on the rear doors or gates used for convenient loading and unloading.

• Wheels and Axles:

Addressing mobility issues by repairing or replacing worn-out, misaligned, or damaged wheels and axles.

• Drainage System:

Maintenance of the drainage system, including the repair or cleaning of drain holes or plugs to ensure proper functionality.

• Sticker:

Relabeling your container.
